Project Overview

Pokémon Card Resale — Financial Performance Analysis

This dashboard analyzes the financial performance of a Pokémon sealed product resale business operated from June through September 2025. It tracks revenue, profit, return on investment, and product-level performance across 211 transactions on eBay, TCGPlayer, and Facebook Marketplace to identify the most profitable inventory decisions, optimal selling platforms, and the impact of product release timing on margin. Raw sales data was collected and validated manually, cleaned in Excel, and visualized here to surface actionable business insights.

Key Business Insights
Launch timing matters: ETBs sold within 72hrs of release yielded 28–35% margins vs. 15–18% after 2 weeks
Platform fee impact: Facebook’s zero-fee model adds ~5.5% net margin vs. eBay’s 12.9% fee structure
Volume vs. margin tradeoff: Booster Bundles drove 45% of transactions but only 30% of total profit
Loss concentration: 10.4% loss rate peaked in August as market prices corrected post-hype
